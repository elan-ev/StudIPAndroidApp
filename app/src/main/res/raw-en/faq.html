Frequently asked questions

  • I can't find my Stud.IP. When will you add mine?

    It's usually more sensible to first ask the Stud.IP support at your university whether they already considered apps or other mobile access for their Stud.IP. For this app to work with your Stud.IP installation we are required for an interface to access the content the system. At all universities we depend on the local Stud.IP maintainer to grant our app access to the Stud.IP interface. Only then can we integrate it into our app and your Stud.IP will usually ship with the next release.

  • I encountered an error in your app. Who do I need to contact to get this fixed?

    There are usually three different ways to get in touch and inform us about it:

    • The build in feedback form.
    • By e-mail at studipmobil@elan-ev.de
    • And for technically oriented people at https://github.com/elan-ev/StudIPAndroidApp/issues
